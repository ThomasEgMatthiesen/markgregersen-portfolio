Mark Ibsgaard Gregersen is a Danish composer, improviser, and bassist. His diverse performance practice includes extended techniques, experimental electronics, and modern and contemporary jazz.

Gregersen has worked with artists including Ned Ferm, Laura Toxværd, Lars-Göran Ulander, Erik Kimestad, Rune Kielsgaard, Nils Bo Davidsen, Lars Vissing, Flavia Huarachi and Margaux Oswald; he also appears on baraboro's cassette 'Coatlicue's dreams', 'Calum Builder's Messe (you are where you need to be)', and Alto Aria's album 'Sowing Worlds'.

In 2020, Gregersen started Dice Club, an improvised music series that serves as a gathering place for musicians and audiences of various ages and experience with live experimental music.

He lives in Copenhagen.
